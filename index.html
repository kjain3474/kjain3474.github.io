About Us

With a presence of more than five decades in the textile industry, Darshan Valji is one of the top players in this segment and has emerged as a clear leader in the Corporate, Industrial & School Uniform segment. With a wide range of varied types of fabrics, sold under the brand name”Valji”, the company’s fabrics are always favored over others when it comes to quality, designs & patterns

A TEXTILE FIRM

100 years back Shri Tekchand Himatmalji Kothari came to Mumbai from a remote village called Bagol in Rajasthan with a dream of realizing his potential in this city. Initially to sustain he worked in various firms in the city which also gave him a wider perspective and a deep understanding about various businesses. In a few years he was joined by his brother Shri Tilokchandji Himatmalji Kothari and both started a Adhat Pedhi in the name of Tilokchand Rajkamal Adat. The business started flourishing and Shri Vastimal Tilokchandji Kothari also joined this family pedi business. Thus the foundation was firmly laid for other ventures to come. Shri Balchandji Kothari, son of Shri Vastimalji Kothari joined the family adat business. In 1981 he started the textile trading company specializing in uniform fabrics. In early 90’s Shri Balchandji Kothari launched the school uniform fabrics manufacturing unit. Since then there has been no looking back, and today Darshan Valji Uniforms is amongst the largest and one of the most respected uniform manufacturing and exporting company of India.
